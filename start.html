Get your daily prep plan

Pay €49, export your sales data, and get item-level prep quantities for the next 7 days — adjusted for weather, updated every morning.

30 days · No subscription · Stops automatically

What you get

  • 30 days of access to your private daily prep plan.
  • 7-day rolling view — today + next 6 days, updated every morning with fresh weather.
  • Item-level quantities — how many of each dish to prep, not just percentages.
  • Busy/quiet highlights — see when a day looks different from normal.
  • Download & share — export as CSV for kitchen, bar, or suppliers.
  • Email support — response within 24–48 hours if you need help.

What you need

  • Sales data from your POS — at least 90 days of item-level sales (CSV or Excel). 12 months is ideal.
  • Your city — so we connect the right weather data.
  • An email address — where we send your upload link and login.

Not sure how to export? Most POS systems do it in a few clicks. We'll send simple instructions after payment — and if you get stuck, we'll help you through it.

Quick questions

What if I can't export from my POS?

We'll help. After payment, you get instructions for common POS systems. If yours isn't listed, email us — we'll figure it out together.

What if it doesn't work for my restaurant?

You've paid €49 for 30 days. At the end, it stops. No subscription to cancel, no ongoing charges. If it helps, continue. If not, you're done.

How long until I can use it?

1 business day after you upload your sales data. Most restaurants are checking their prep plan by the next morning.
